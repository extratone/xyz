Slot 5Monster #34
Original release: 2004–2005
A gripping, dramatic tale of life, death, consequence, and revenge. Genius neurosurgeon Kenzou Tenma, young and promising in his early career, saved the life of a young boy who had suffered a gunshot to the head, despite the hospital’s Director wanting him to instead operate on a more politically important patient. Dr. Tenma knew that it was the right thing to do… but later, he was forced to question that and many other choices….
Slot 6Spice and Wolf II #6
Original release: 2009
A tale of a travelling merchant aspiring to riches and a forsaken harvest goddess longing to return to her homeland. Drama, adventure, political intrigue, and economics. Highly recommended!
Slot 7Ryoko’s Case File #7
Original release: 2008
Ryoko investigates mysterious mysteries!
Slot 8Armored Trooper VOTOMS #34
Original release: 1983–1984
A classic mecha series from the 1980s. This is the third story arc.
Slot 9Chobits #19
Original release: 2002
The popular CLAMP series featuring Chi. Computers that look like people (women, mostly). This series was requested several times last year, so presumably a lot of you want to see it!
